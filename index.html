"A Dream Fulfilled: India Clinches the 2024 T20 World Cup After 13-Year's"

Published on: August 20, 2024

India ended their ICC trophy drought by defeating South Africa in the ICC T20 World Cup 2024 final by only seven runs on Saturday at the Kensington Oval, Bridgetown, Barbados.

There were emotional scenes after India won the T20 World Cup title on Saturday by defeating South Africa, with captain Rohit Sharma and team Virat Kohli. Virat Kohli was named Player of the Match for scoring 76 runs off 59 balls, while Bumrah won Player of the Tournament for taking 15 wickets at an economy of 4.17.

    Apple introduces iPhone 16 and iPhone 16 Plus

    Published on: September 9, 2024

    Apple today announced iPhone 16 and iPhone 16 Plus, built for Apple Intelligence, the easy-to-use personal intelligence system that understands personal context to deliver intelligence that is helpful and relevant while protecting user privacy. The iPhone 16 lineup also introduces Camera Control, which brings new ways to capture memories, and will help users quickly access visual intelligence to learn about objects or places around them faster than ever before.

    iPhone 16 and iPhone 16 Plus will be available in five bold colours: black, white, pink, teal, and ultramarine. Pre-orders begin Friday, September 13, with availability beginning Friday, September 20.

      Deepika Padukone, Ranveer Singh welcome baby girl

      Published on: September 8, 2024

      Bollywood's beloved power couple,Deepika Padukone and Ranveer Singh, are celebrating the arrival of their first child, a baby girl, on Sunday, September 8. The news, shared by sources close to the family, has brought joy to their fans across the world. Deepika was admitted to Mumbai’s HN Reliance Foundation Hospital on Saturday evening in preparation for the delivery, and the couple arrived at the hospital accompanied by their families.

      The happy couple shared the exciting news of their first child’s arrival Sunday via social media. They posted a joint message that read:"Welcome baby girl. 8.09.2024. Deepika and Ranveer."
